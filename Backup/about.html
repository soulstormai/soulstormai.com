About SoulstormAI

Welcome to SoulstormAI, where we build apps that actually solve problems. No buzzwords. No hype. Just practical tools that make everyday life easier.

Our Story

SoulstormAI was founded in 2024 out of frustration with the current state of mobile applications. Too many apps promise everything and deliver nothing. They're bloated with features nobody uses, riddled with ads, and designed to waste your time rather than save it.

We decided to do things differently. Every app we build starts with a real problem that we've personally experienced. We don't build solutions looking for problems—we solve problems that genuinely frustrate people.

"Most apps are solutions looking for problems. We do the opposite. We find real problems that real people have, then build the simplest possible solution."

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: Build apps that people actually use. Not apps that look good in screenshots. Not apps that win design awards. Apps that solve specific problems and get out of your way.

We achieve this by following these principles:

  • Problem-First Approach: We only build apps that solve problems we've personally encountered.
  • Simplicity Over Features: We resist feature creep. If it doesn't solve the core problem, it doesn't ship.
  • Respect for Users: No dark patterns, no manipulation, no wasting your time.
  • Privacy-Focused: We collect the minimum data necessary and never sell your information.
  • Fair Pricing: Free tiers that actually work. Premium features that are worth paying for.
  • Quality Over Quantity: We'd rather have 5 excellent apps than 50 mediocre ones.

What Makes Us Different

We Use Our Own Apps.

Every app we build is something we use ourselves. PetWander exists because we got tired of spending hours researching pet-friendly hotels. TipSplit exists because we couldn't find a bill-splitting app that let us split the bill one way and the tip another.

No Venture Capital.

We're 100% bootstrapped. This means we answer to our users, not investors. We don't have to chase unrealistic growth targets or compromise our values to please shareholders. Our only goal is to build apps that people love.

Transparency.

We're honest about what our apps can and can't do. We don't make exaggerated claims or use misleading marketing. If something doesn't work perfectly, we tell you.

AI Where It Helps.

We use artificial intelligence where it genuinely improves the user experience, not as a marketing gimmick. Our AI features serve real purposes—personalized recommendations, smart search, predictive features—not just buzzword compliance.

Our Apps

PetWander

Travel planning for pet owners. We built PetWander after spending countless hours researching pet-friendly accommodations for every trip. Now you can find pet-friendly hotels, restaurants, parks, and emergency vets in one place. Plan your entire trip in minutes instead of hours.

TipSplit

Bill splitting calculator with advanced features. Most bill-splitting apps make you choose: split everything equally or calculate each person's exact items. We added a third option: split the bill one way and the tip another way. Perfect for when only some people want to tip.

Oracle

AI-powered divination combining tarot, I-Ching, and runes. Currently in development. We saw people paying $50+ for single tarot readings and thought there had to be a better way. Oracle provides unlimited readings for one monthly subscription.

Business Model

We believe in sustainable business models that align our interests with yours. Our apps use a combination of:

  • Freemium Model: Core features free forever. Premium features available for subscription.
  • One-Time Purchases: For apps where subscriptions don't make sense.
  • Advertising: Minimal, non-intrusive ads in free versions (Google AdSense).
  • Affiliate Partnerships: We recommend tools we actually use and earn a commission when you sign up.

We never use dark patterns to extract money from users. Our pricing is straightforward, and you always know exactly what you're paying for.
