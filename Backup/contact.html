What to Include in Your Message

To help us assist you faster, please include:

  • Which app you're contacting about (PetWander, TipSplit, Oracle, etc.)
  • Your device (iPhone, Android, web browser, etc.)
  • What happened (screenshots are very helpful)
  • What you expected to happen
  • Steps to reproduce the issue (if applicable)

Common Questions

Q: How do I report a bug?

Email us at info@soulstormai.com with details about the bug, including your device, app version, and steps to reproduce it. Screenshots help tremendously.

Q: How do I request a new feature?

We love hearing feature ideas! Email us your suggestion. While we can't implement every request, we seriously consider all feedback.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings in the app. If you need help, email us and we'll walk you through it.

Q: Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer refunds within 30 days of purchase if you're not satisfied. Just email us explaining why and we'll process your refund.

Q: Can I partner with you?

We're always open to partnerships that align with our values. Email us with details about your proposal.
