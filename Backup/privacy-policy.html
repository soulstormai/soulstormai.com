QuantumSplit Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2026

QuantumSplit ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

QuantumSplit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All video processing occurs locally on your device.

Data Usage

The app accesses your device storage solely to:

• Read video files you select for splitting
• Save split video clips to your device

No data leaves your device. No analytics, tracking, or telemetry is used.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. There is no data to share.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since no data is collected, there is nothing to retain or delete. Video files remain under your control on your device.

Permissions

The app requests storage permission only to read and write video files. This data stays on your device.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted here with a new date.

Contact

For questions, contact us at your website or app store listing.
