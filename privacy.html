Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

SoulstormAI ("we," "our," or "us") operates multiple software applications and this website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services, including but not limited to PetWander, TipSplit, Oracle, and any future applications.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and password.
  • Profile Information: Optional information such as your name, profile picture, and preferences.
  • User Content: Search queries, saved locations, preferences, and any content you create using our apps.
  • Payment Information: When you make a purchase, payment information is processed securely through Stripe. We never store your full credit card details.
  • Communications: Messages you send to our support team or feedback you provide.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, clicks, and navigation patterns.
  • Location Data: Precise location (with your permission) for location-based features like finding pet-friendly places in PetWander.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect usage data and improve your experience.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our services.

Information from Third Parties:

  • Social Media: If you connect your social media accounts, we receive basic profile information.
  • Payment Processors: Transaction confirmations and payment status from Stripe.
  • Business Partners: Information from affiliate partners when you use their services through our links.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: Operate, maintain, and improve our applications and website.
  • Personalization: Customize your experience based on your preferences and usage patterns.
  • Communication: Send important updates, security alerts, and respond to your inquiries.
  • Payment Processing: Process transactions and manage subscriptions.
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns, identify bugs, and improve our services.
  • Marketing: Send promotional materials (you can opt-out anytime).
  • Security: Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads through Google AdSense and measure ad performance.

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share information in these circumstances:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe processes payments on our behalf.

  • Advertising Partners: Google AdSense displays ads on our site.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms.
  • Payment Security: Payment data is handled by PCI-compliant processors (Stripe).
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal information.
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and system updates.
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for suspicious activity.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
